
Convivia Work Website
Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Version: 1.1 Company: Convivia Work Tagline: Create Connect CoWork

1. Product Overview
Develop a tech-forward marketing website for Convivia Work that:
	•	Showcases the coworking space
	•	Converts visitors into members
	•	Integrates with the CoWorks SaaS backend
The website will function primarily as a frontend marketing and conversion layer while CoWorks handles operational backend functionality such as:
	•	membership management
	•	booking
	•	payments
	•	CRM
	•	space management
The website should appeal to:
	•	startup founders
	•	freelancers
	•	small businesses
	•	highly productive professionals

2. Vision
Create a modern, technology-forward coworking website that communicates:
	•	productivity
	•	innovation
	•	collaboration
Convivia Work should be positioned as:
• a technology-first coworking environment • a community for builders and creators • a workspace for highly productive professionals
The brand journey is:
Create → Connect → CoWork
This should guide the messaging and layout of the website.

3. Goals

8. Workspace & Amenities
The workspace environment should highlight flexibility for different work styles and moods, emphasizing both energy and focus.
The messaging should communicate that Convivia Work supports both high-energy collaboration and deep focused productivity.
Members can move between environments depending on their workflow and mood.

Natural Light Collaboration Areas
Open workspace areas feature large windows and abundant natural light, creating an energizing environment for:
	•	collaboration
	•	brainstorming
	•	casual meetings
	•	creative thinking
These spaces encourage connection and interaction between builders, founders, and creators.

Focused Glare-Free Work Zones
Dedicated work areas are designed specifically for deep focus and screen-heavy work.
Features include:
	•	glare-free lighting conditions
	•	optimized desk layouts
	•	minimal distractions
	•	comfortable long-session work environments
Ideal for:
	•	developers
	•	graphic designers
	•	videographers and editors
	•	artists 
Technology-Enabled Conference Rooms
Conference rooms include:
	•	full video conferencing
	•	large displays
	•	high-speed connectivity
	•	presentation-ready setups
Ideal for:
	•	client meetings
	•	remote collaboration
	•	team strategy sessions

Podcast Studio
A dedicated podcast and recording studio available for:
	•	podcast production
	•	interviews
	•	video recording
	•	media content creation

Key Amenities
• enterprise-grade internet • video-enabled conference rooms • podcast studio • creative glare-free workspace • natural light collaboration areas • quiet focus zones • community gathering spaces
