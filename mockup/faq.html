General

Convivia Work is a technology-first coworking space in Walnut Creek, CA, built for founders, creatives, and highly productive professionals. The space features glare-free focus zones, natural light collaboration areas, fully equipped conference rooms, and a professional-grade AV production studio — all running on multigigabit infrastructure with 5G failover.

Membership

We offer four plans:

Day Pass — $50/day. Adjustable standing desk, monitor, kitchen and coffee access, and phone booth access.

Coworking Membership — $382/month. 24/7 access, conference room credits, and priority invites to community and founder events.

Dedicated Desk Membership — $450/month. Everything in Coworking, plus a permanent personalized workstation with lockable storage and mail service eligibility.

Custom Team Suite — Custom pricing for teams of 2–12, with a private dedicated space and full access to shared amenities.
